Who is Uncle Scary?

For over a 3 years now the Youtube channel Uncle Scary Has been making complanitary video for old and obscure horror films


The videos feature Uncle Scary consists of presenting, hosting intermissions, or delivering closing remarks for a particular movie in a simple and corny way, however the due to Uncle Scary's heavy accent and bad aduio quality it is ususly hard to make out what is been said,


But the most peculiar thing about the Uncle Scary Youtube channel is the amount of videos it has, a channel like this, how many video do you think it has?

10? 20? 50? perhaps 100?


would ypu belive me if I said 14,446 videos?

This is a warning to those who meet up with me, you are going to leave the comfort of the light and set in to the word of the supernatural
